About this game

The game is set in the year 2033, twenty years after a devastating pandemic caused by a parasitic fungus known as Cordyceps. This outbreak has transformed much of the human population into aggressive, zombie-like creatures driven by primal instincts and an insatiable hunger.


Society has crumbled in the wake of the infection. Cities lie in ruins, nature slowly reclaims abandoned buildings, and survivors live under the constant threat of both the infected and ruthless human factions. The remaining government operates through heavily militarized quarantine zones, often prioritizing control over compassion.


Amid this chaos, the story follows characters who struggle to maintain their humanity in a world stripped of order and hope. As players explore the bleak and dangerous landscape, they must face moral dilemmas, forge unlikely bonds, and fight for survival in a world where trust is a rare and precious thing.
