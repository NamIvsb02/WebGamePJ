About this game

Forza Horizon 5 takes you on a breathtaking open-world racing adventure across the diverse landscapes of Mexico — from dense jungles and ancient ruins to sun-drenched beaches and snow-capped volcanoes. With dynamic seasons and weather, every race feels fresh, and every road tells a new story.


Whether you're drifting through dusty desert trails, speeding along vibrant city streets, or launching off cliffs in custom-built supercars, the game gives you complete freedom to explore, compete, and create. With hundreds of cars, seamless multiplayer, and community-driven events, Forza Horizon 5 is a celebration of speed, creativity, and the thrill of the open road.
