About this game

Kratos' revenge on the Olympians is long past. Now, Kratos lives in seclusion as a common man in a land where Norse gods and monsters rule. But in this harsh, unforgiving world, he must fight to survive… and teach his son the same.


Kratos is once again a father. As the mentor and protector of Atreus, his son is eager to prove himself and earn his respect, Kratos must control his natural rage as he ventures into a dangerous world alongside his son.


From the towering marbles and pillars of Mount Olympus to the barren forests, mountains, and caves of pre-Viking Norse lore, this is a brand new realm filled with unique creatures, monsters, and gods. Plunge into


With an over-the-shoulder camera that puts players closer to the action than ever before, God of War™ combat reflects the true form of the Norse creatures Kratos will face: massive, ferocious, and brutal. New primary weapons and new abilities retain the hallmarks of the God of War series, while delivering a perspective on conflict that breaks new ground in the genre.
