About this game

The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt is set in a vast, war-torn world teetering between beauty and brutality. In this dark fantasy epic, players step into the boots of Geralt of Rivia, a hardened monster hunter known as a witcher, gifted with supernatural abilities and cursed with difficult choices.


The Northern Kingdoms are in chaos. Armies clash, kings fall, and an otherworldly threat—the spectral Wild Hunt—scours the land in search of Geralt's lost ward, Ciri, a girl of prophecy with unimaginable power. As war and ancient magic ravage the continent, Geralt must traverse cities steeped in corruption, forests crawling with beasts, and mountains echoing with forgotten legends.


With a rich open world filled with morally complex characters, intricate political intrigue, and visceral combat, The Witcher 3 challenges players not just to fight monsters—but to confront the monsters within. Every decision shapes the fate of nations, and the line between good and evil is never clear.
