About this game

Hearts of Iron IV puts you at the helm of a nation during the most turbulent time in modern history — World War II. As the world descends into global conflict, you must lead your country through war, diplomacy, and industrial might, shaping the course of history through strategy and sacrifice.


Whether you choose to rewrite history or relive it, the fate of the world lies in your hands. Build vast armies, command fleets and air wings, form alliances or crush them — every decision, from battlefield tactics to political maneuvering, will determine victory or defeat on a global scale.
